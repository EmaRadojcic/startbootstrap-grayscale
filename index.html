TWO WAYS TO PLAY!

VR Box Controls

To play with controller, make sure bluetooth pairing is enabled and controller is in VR mode (c+@) Fire bow/interact:Top trigger, change bow state: Bottom trigger, Menu: "C", Walk: Joystick forward

Google Cardboard

To play with voice controlls make sure microphone permissions are enabled on the phone. Fire bow/interact: say "shoot"/press google cardboard box button, change bow state: say "lightning" or "fire", Menu: say "menu", Walk: LookDown
